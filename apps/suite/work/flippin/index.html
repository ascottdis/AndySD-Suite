The Problem

Resale workflows are fragmented chaos: items tracked in spreadsheets, comps researched in 12 browser tabs, listing drafts scattered across marketplaces, and zero clean loop from "I sourced this" → "It sold."

Pain 01
No single source of truth for inventory
Pain 02
Listing = slow manual copy-paste hell
Pain 03
No ROI clarity until item ships

The Solution

outFLIP makes inventory the single source of truth. Every item becomes a living record: sourced date, cost, comps, AI-generated listings, multi-channel sync, and sell-through tracking.

Feature 01
Inventory Core

One database for all items: photos, cost basis, condition notes, marketplace status, location tracking.

Feature 02
AutoList

Gemini AI drafts titles, descriptions, tags from photos + item data. One-click to eBay/Mercari/Poshmark templates.

Feature 03
LiveSell

Real-time status per marketplace: listed, viewed, offered, sold. Full sell-through visibility.

Feature 04
Comps + ROI

Automated comp research, margin calculator, velocity insights. Know profitability before you list.
